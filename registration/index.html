Information for Authors

All extended abstracts must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another conference or publication avenues. Authors are required to prepare an extended abstract(s) in 2 pages (at most) and submit it via https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=fimimipumso2021. A template of extended abstract is available here. The extended abstract will be reviewed by the conference committee. All accepted extended abstracts will be published via the conference website.

This Joint Conference is free for all authors of extended abstract, but registration is required for presentation in Zoom. Guidelines for conference registration will be provided soon.

Authors with high-quality presentation during the conference will also be invited to extend their extended abstract for submission to special issues of several international journals, as follows.

  • 1. FIM JCISS v46, free to publish.
  • 2. FIM JCISS v47, free to publish.
  • 3. Journal of Advanced Computational Intelligence and Intelligent Informatics (JACIII), special issue of Scopus journal: OPEN ACCESS, visit here.
  • 4. MDPI FinTech, special issue: Advanced Financial Technologies: OPEN ACCESS 10 best papers are free to publish, visit here.
  • 5. Mathematica Japonica ISSN 0025-5513, free to publish, Published bimonthly by Japanese Association of Mathematical Sciences.
  • 6. More special issues to be announced.

Important Dates

  • 1. Submission of Extended Abstract: 20 November 2021
  • 2. Review outcome of Extended Abstract: Within 2 weeks
  • 3. Conference Registration (free of charge): 15 December 2021
